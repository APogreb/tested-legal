Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Tested is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data — in plain language.

1. Who We Are

Tested is an independent AI tools review channel publishing short-form video content on TikTok, Instagram, and related platforms. References to "we", "us", or "Tested" in this policy refer to the Tested channel and its operators.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect minimal data. Here is what may be collected depending on how you interact with us:

Data Type When Collected Purpose
Email address If you contact us directly To respond to your enquiry
Platform analytics When you view or interact with our content Understanding audience reach (aggregated, anonymised)
TikTok/Instagram profile data When you authorise our TikTok app integration To enable automated content posting via official APIs only

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

3. TikTok API Data Usage

Tested uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish video content to our own TikTok account. In connection with this integration:

  • We only access data necessary to post content to our own account
  • We do not collect, store, or process personal data belonging to TikTok users
  • We do not access follower data, private messages, or any user account information
  • API credentials are stored securely and never shared with third parties
  • Our use of the TikTok API complies with TikTok's Platform Policy and Developer Terms

4. Third-Party Platforms

Our content is hosted on third-party platforms including TikTok and Instagram. When you interact with our content on those platforms, their own privacy policies apply. We encourage you to review:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Instagram / Meta Privacy Policy

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the privacy practices of those platforms.

5. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies beyond what may be provided by the hosting platform. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tracking tools on this legal page.

6. How We Store Your Data

Any personal data you voluntarily share with us (e.g. via email) is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was provided. We do not store payment information.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

If you are based in the UK or European Union, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to object — object to how we process your data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Tested content is intended for general audiences aged 13 and over, in accordance with TikTok and Instagram platform policies. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify followers of significant changes via our social media channels and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our content after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
